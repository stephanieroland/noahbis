FAQ

What's inside a Noah kit?
Your Noah Kit contains 72-hours supplies for you and your family. The kit is packed with all the family (radio, multitool, ...) and individual items (food, water, flashlights, ...). To discover every included product, click here.
More than 2 people in your household? No problem.
Your Noah Kit will then be shipped into several boxes containing the additional individual items. Nevertheless, you may choose the size of your boxes (see question hereunder).
How many boxes do we receive for a family?
When ordering your Noah Box, you'll be asked during the checkout process to choose between two types of boxes: a 1-person underbed box or a 2-person walk-in closet box. The dimensions of each of the boxes and their design will be shown to you at the same time to help you choose wisely.
Depending on your selection, you may receive as many boxes as your household account, or 2-person boxes.
How much does the shipping cost?
Nothing. Shipping is free inside the United States.

Our return policy is very simple: you're not satisfied? Return your box and you'll get your money back. You only have to pay for the shipping costs.
